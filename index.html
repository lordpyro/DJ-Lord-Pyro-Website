Lord Pyro

Lord Pyro is a DJ/Producer from the 7th Galaxy descendent from a long line of musical Pharoahs living in Austin TX.

He has opened for Arc Attack, and has had a one man show at the Alamo Drafthouse Cinema complete with 90 minute video.

His sets include Live Improvisatory PA, along with DJ controllerism with a range that includes Tech House, Deep House, Future House, and Bass Music. His influences range from world beat to synth-pop

He has shared the decks with Dj's from Opulent Temple, and The DO Lab. Selected DJ Performances Include: Burning Man, SXSW, Austin Fashion Week
